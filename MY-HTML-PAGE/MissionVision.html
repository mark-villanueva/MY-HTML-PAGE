Polytechnic University of the Philippines

VISION

PUP Santa maria envisions to offer the students of the municipality quality, relevant, responsive, and
excellent tertiary education aimed to produce graduates that are future leaders and expert in
their chosen field of endeavor and contributes to the full development of the family, community,
society, and the nation at large.

MISSION

To provide high quality undergraduate program and globally competitive human capital development and community services accessible particularly to the students of the municipality.

GOALS AND OBJECTIVES

To provide quality tertiary education relevant to the industry requirements of globalzation.

To offer curricula responsive to the needs of the industry and society.

To enhance the development of the critical thinking and mental growth of the client

To inculcate social awareness and desirable values to the minds of every student

To develop partnerships and linkages with industries

To ensure the highest quality and affordable instructions to the clients

To produce globally competitive graduates
